Item Containers

Item Containers are the primary items this mod adds. They have 6 main stats which can be seen by holding Shift while hovering over the stack with your mouse.

  • Slot count: the amount of slots it has.
  • Slot Capacity: how many items a slot can hold
  • Allowed Size: what's the largest TFC Size allowed in the slots.

  • Item Pickup if it can pickup item entities from the world. Shift right click or use to toggle.
  • Item Voiding if it can void excess items picked up from the world. To toggle Ctrl + Left Click slots.
  • Inventory interaction if you can insert and extract from it by clicking on the stack like TFC vessels or vanilla bundles.

All Item Containers support vanilla pick block via

For modpack authors or simply people wanting to configure things. All Item Containers have a "blacklist" tag which includes sns:prevented_in_item_containers. The naming scheme is prevented_in_ for example the straw basket uses sns:prevented_in_straw_basket. Item Containers with special "whitelist" tags will have them listed in their book entry.

Biomes

The Beneath has a full set of new biomes to explore! Most are split into two categories: upper and lower. The upper biomes are only found higher up, while the lower ones are closer to the lava.

Lush Hollow

Humid and full of greenery! The most hospitable area for an underground base.

Decaying Caverns

Nature's recyclers are hard at work in this damp region full of rot.

Webbed Lair

No prizes for guessing what lives here! Also features huge Travertine pillars.

Ash Forest

What remains of the Lush Hollow after the frequent fires caused by the heat.

Lava Floes

An ore-rich Pyroxenite area crisscrossed with rivers of lava.

Basalt Deltas

Everyone's favourite nether biome returns, and is just as dangerous to navigate as ever.

Deep Caves

Between the other biomes are more plain areas of either Gabbro, Schist, Granite, Diorite, and Gneiss.

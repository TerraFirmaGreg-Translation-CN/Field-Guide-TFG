Piglins

Piglin Bartering

Like you'd expect, you can trade with Piglins in the same way you trade with them in vanilla, with Gold Ingots.

Piglin

You can find Piglins in almost every biome throughout the Beneath.

Bastions

Raiding a Bastion might be very dangerous, but they can also be very rewarding! You can find things like:

  • Gold, Rose Gold, Pig Iron, Black Bronze, and Silver ingots
  • Black Bronze weapons, tools, and armor
  • Kaolin clay and graphite
  • Permanent light sources
  • Cloth, leather, seeds, and food
  • And other treasures!

If you want to ensure an easy time trading--or simply mingling with the locals--Put on a Piglin Disguise to prevent Piglins from being hostile with you on first sight. But beware! They only put up with so much...

ArborFirmaCraft

If you see Tualang or Fig trees in your travels, you'll be pleased to know that you'll be able to create Boats with them just like any other kind of Wood

Softwoods have sufficiently soft enough pulp and bark that they're perfect for creating Dugout Canoes

  • Tualang
  • Fig
  • Cypress

Known for their exceptional sturdiness, Hardwood trees are ideal for creating larger and sophisticated Watercrafts such as Rowboats and Sloops

  • Baobab
  • Eucalyptus
  • Mahogany
  • Hevea
  • Teak
  • Ironwood
  • Ipe
